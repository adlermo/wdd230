PARC: Repetition

Github Profile Page

https://github.com/adlermo

The page as shown above presents repetition technique — among other principles of good design — on the cards along the page. Each of them presents a title, a description and the dominant technology on the repositories and, by doing so it creates a pleasant and easy pattern to the users.

Fitt's Law

Microsoft Learn Platform

https://docs.microsoft.com/

Accessing Microsoft Learning Platform with an personal account we can see Fitt's Law by the position of "Resume" buttons, which are placed on the right (closer to the thumb) and in a bigger button. Their positioning enhance time on pressing forward on studies and I believe it encourages users to keep learning at the same.

Hick's Law

Olhar Digital News Website

https://olhardigital.com.br/

Solving the problem of ads using an ad blocker, we'll realize Hick's law acting in the page. As a tech news web portal Olhar Digital removed from its header anything but their logo making content around the info article less distractive and easier to make choices (scroll down). Their home page does the same by showing only 2 articles in a row.
